What differentiates a fountain pen from normal pens?

Fountain pens work in a different way than other pens such as ballpoint pens and rollerball pens. Looking at the visual of a fountain pen, you can see that there is a striking difference between them. They work as a controlled leakage. To fully understand the workings of a fountain pen, click here.

Why should I use a fountain pen?

First of all, fountain pens do not require force upon them for them to spew forth ink. This means that for long periods of writing, there is less stress in writing with a fountain pen. More reasons on Fountain pens also have nib customizations that will suit the handwriting and needs of a specific person. More on nib customizations here.

Why should I pay so much for a pen?

A fountain pen is not just a pen, it is a piece of art. Some fountain pens such as Nakaya’s and urushi fountain pens are truly art at its finest. Treat such fountain pens as if you would treat and artwork: admire it. Well made fountain pens such as those can still be used and should be used. Fountain pens, though art, are still instruments of writing. If you feel uncomfortable with spending your money on a 40 dollar fountain pen, then you can easily go on Amazon to get some cheap Chinese fountain pens. You can then move on to more expensive pens if you like it.
